LumiSpire

Explore. Learn. Level up.

A world-exploring game where every adventure teaches a real skill — and all your progress stays encrypted on your own Solid Pod, so it belongs to you, not a game company.

In plain words (for anyone, any age): It's a game world you explore. Solve puzzles to earn coins and level up, unlock new lands, and dress up your character — while secretly getting smarter. And your game save is locked in a box only you can open. 🔒

Your data stays yours (Solid)

1. Reads & writes to your Pod

Your progress is saved to and loaded from your own Solid Pod — there's no LumiSpire server keeping it.

2. Encryption by default

Progress is written with writePod(encrypted: true), so the Pod stores a scrambled file only your key can open.

3. Consent & sharing you control (WAC/ACP)

Consent is explicit (with a parental-consent switch for kids). You can give a teacher or guardian read-only access with grantPermission and take it back with revokePermission anytime.

4. Data minimisation

Only your game progress is stored. Nothing is sold, ever.

Try everything in one tap

Log in, then open My Explorer → "Load demo save" to fill your Pod with coins, progress and cosmetics so every feature is live instantly.

For developers

  • Flutter/Dart on solidpod + solidui.
  • Progress is one encrypted Turtle document (lumispire_progress.ttl) on the player's Pod.
  • Sharing uses Solid WAC/ACP; login is Solid-OIDC (see client-profile.jsonld).
  • Pure progression rules are unit-tested (test/progression_test.dart).
